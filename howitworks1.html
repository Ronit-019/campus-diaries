How It Works

Bringing the Podcast to Life in 3 Easy Steps

At Campus Diaries, we’ve simplified the process of creating your own podcast. Whether you’re new to podcasting or already experienced, our platform is designed to make your journey smooth and enjoyable. Here’s how you can get started

1. Book Your Session

Ready to get started? Head over to our Booking Page, select a date and time that works for you, and reserve your podcasting slot. Our integrated calendar shows all available times, so you can choose a session that fits your schedule. Once booked, you’ll receive a confirmation email with everything you need to know about your session.

2. Record Your Episode

On the day of your session, join us in our state-of-the-art studio (or connect remotely) to record your episode. We provide all the equipment and support you need to create a professional-quality podcast. If you’re recording remotely, don’t worry—our platform ensures high-quality audio from any location. You can focus on your content while we handle the technical side!

3. Edit and Publish

After your session, you can choose to edit your episode yourself using our easy-to-use tools or have one of our editors polish it for you. Once your podcast is ready, we’ll help you publish it on all major platforms—Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, and more. Share your creation with the world!
